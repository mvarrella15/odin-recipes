Recipe: Pizza

Description

For this recipe, if followed correctly, will guide you through the process of making a delicious cheese pizza. This recipe will provide you with a list of ingredients needed, as well as a step by step guide to making a pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Flour
  • Basil, if desired

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Start by taking dough, and kneading it in flower on a table or other clean surface
  4. Once kneaded, take a rolling pin and make the dough into a thin, flat circle
  5. Take a ladle, and apply sauce on pizza, spreading it evenly
  6. Apply cheese evenly on top, until entire pizza is covered
  7. For this step, you can apply small amounts of basil leaves on top
  8. Place pizza in oven for 14 minutes
  9. After finished cooking, slice pizza into desired slice size
  10. Enjoy!
