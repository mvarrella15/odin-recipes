Recipe: Lemon Herb Chicken

Description

For this recipe, if followed correctly, will guide you through the process of making a delicious lemon herb chicken dish. This recipe will provide you with a list of ingredients needed, as well as a step by step guide to making this dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 5 oz. skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced, divided
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch dried oregano
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a bowl; pour 1/2 of the lemon juice over chicken and season with salt
  2. Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Place chicken into hot oil. Add remaining lemon juice and oregano; season with black pepper.
  4. Cook chicken until golden brown and the juices run clear, 5 to 10 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
  5. Garnish chicken with parsley to serve.
  6. Enjoy!
