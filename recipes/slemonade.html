Recipe: Strawberry Lemonade

Description

A nice, refreshing glass of strawberry lemonade. The best part about this recipe is the fact it is all naturally sourced ingredients. If you follow this recipe exactly as listed, you'll have this refreshing drink in 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 8 large strawberries, halved
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 7 cups water, divided
  • 2 cups freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Place strawberries in a blender; top with 2 tablespoons sugar.
  2. Pour 1 cup water over sugared strawberries; blend until completely smooth
  3. Transfer strawberry juice into a large pitcher; stir in remaining 6 cups water, lemon juice, and 1 cup sugar until well blended.
  4. Chill before serving.
  5. Enjoy!
